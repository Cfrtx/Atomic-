Safety & Security

Atomic Executor is completely safe to use. Like all Roblox executors, it may be flagged by antivirus software because it injects into the Roblox process - this is normal behavior and not indicative of malware. Our software contains no malicious code, doesn't steal information, and is open for inspection by any security researcher. The detections you see are false positives caused by the injection method required for Roblox scripting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Atomic safe to use?
+
Yes, Atomic is completely safe. The antivirus detections are false positives caused by the injection method required for Roblox scripting. We do not collect any personal information or include malicious code.
Why is my antivirus flagging Atomic?
+
Antivirus programs detect any program that modifies another process (like Roblox) as potentially dangerous. This is a standard detection for all executors and doesn't mean Atomic is actually harmful. You can safely add an exception for Atomic in your antivirus settings.
Does Atomic require a key?
+
No, Atomic is completely keyless. Unlike other executors that require keys or have HWID locks, Atomic can be used immediately after download without restrictions.
